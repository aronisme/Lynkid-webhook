Privacy Policy

Gemini Prompt Generator

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Gemini Prompt Generator" Chrome extension (the "Extension") collects, uses, and shares your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use the authentication features, the Extension uses Google OAuth2 to verify your identity. We may collect your:

  • Email address
  • Profile information (name, profile picture)

This information is used solely to manage your account, verify subscription status (Freemium/Premium), and provide access to features.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

The Extension interacts with web pages you visit only when you explicitly trigger features like "Scrape Images" or use the context menu. It collects:

  • Image URLs and dimensions from the active tab.
  • Page titles or metadata needed for prompt generation.

Data from your web pages is processed locally and only shared with necessary third-party AI providers during active generation tasks.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core functionality: identifying images and generating AI prompts.
  • Verify your subscription status and grant appropriate access.
  • Improve the Extension's performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • API Keys: Any personal API keys you provide (Gemini or Groq) are stored locally on your device via Chrome's secure storage APIs. They are never transmitted to our private servers.
  • User Account Info: Your login state and subscription data are handled securely via Firebase (Google Cloud Infrastructure).
  • Image Data: We do not store the images you process on our servers. Images are sent directly to AI providers (Google/Groq) via encrypted connections for real-time analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension communicates with the following services:

  • Google Gemini API: To analyze image content.
  • Groq API: An alternative provider for image/text analysis.
  • Firebase: For secure authentication and database management.

Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these providers for information on how they handle data.

5. Your Choices

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Extension. You may also log out at any time to remove your authentication token and cease account-related data processing. You have the right to request the deletion of your account data by contacting us.
