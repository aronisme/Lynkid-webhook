Privacy Policy

Scraper Prompt Generator

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Scraper Prompt Generator" Chrome extension (the "Extension") collects, handles, stores, and shares your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

1. Data Collection (What we collect)

We collect the following types of data when you use the Extension:

  • Personal/Authentication Information: When you log in, we collect your Email Address, Name, Profile Picture, and Google User ID via Google OAuth2.
  • Website Content (Images & Metadata): When you explicitly click "Scrape Images" or use the context menu, the Extension reads Image URLs, alt texts, dimensions, and page titles from your active tab.
  • User-Provided Configuration: Any third-party API Keys (e.g., Gemini API Key, Groq API Key) you manually enter into the Extension's settings.
  • Usage Statistics: We track the number of images you process specifically to manage Freemium/Premium tier limits.

2. Data Handling (How we process your data)

We handle your data securely to provide the Extension's core features:

  • Image & Page Data: Handled entirely locally in your browser. When you generate a prompt, this visual data is sent directly to the selected AI provider (Google Gemini or Groq) to be analyzed. It is never routed through or processed by our own servers.
  • API Keys: Handled securely by the Chrome browser. They are used exclusively to authenticate your requests directly with your chosen AI provider.
  • Permissions: The Extension requires host_permissions for all URLs (<all_urls>) solely to enable image extraction on any website you choose to analyze. The Extension only accesses a page when you explicitly activate it on that specific tab.

3. Data Storage (Where your data lives)

Our data storage practices are designed to maximize your privacy:

  • Local Storage: Your third-party API Keys and Extension settings are stored strictly locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. We do not store your API keys on our servers.
  • Cloud Storage (Authentication): Your user profile (email, name) and subscription status (Freemium/Premium limits) are stored securely on Google Firebase infrastructure.
  • Zero Retention for Media: We do not store, save, or log the images, website content, or generated AI prompts on any of our servers or databases.

4. Data Sharing (Who we share data with)

We strictly govern who receives your data:

  • We DO NOT sell your data: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information, browsing history, or scraped images to any third parties for advertising or any other purpose.
  • Third-Party Processors: To provide AI capabilities, the Extension transmits necessary data (image URLs or content) directly to the AI service you select (Google Gemini or Groq) over encrypted connections. This sharing only occurs when you initiate a prompt generation.
  • Firebase (Google): Used exclusively to manage your user authentication and subscription lifecycle.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You maintain full control over your data. You can:

  • Revoke Access: Log out at any time within the Extension to remove your authentication token and stop account syncing.
  • Clear Data: Clear your Chrome local storage or uninstall the Extension to permanently delete saved API keys and local settings.
  • Request Deletion: You have the right to request the complete deletion of your user account and associated subscription data. Please contact us to fulfill this request.
